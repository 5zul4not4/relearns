Terms and Conditions

1. Tutor selection: Clearly state the qualifications, expertise, and experience of the tutor who will be providing the home tuition services.
2. Services provided: Outline the specific subjects or areas of study that the tutor will cover during the home tuition sessions.
3. Schedule and duration: Specify the number of sessions per week, their duration (e.g., one hour), and the start and end dates of the tuition period.
4. Location: Clearly state that the tuition will take place at the student's home and provide the address.
5. Payments: Clearly outline the payment terms, including the hourly rate or monthly fee and the preferred method of payment.
6. Cancellations and rescheduling: Explain the policy for cancelling or rescheduling tuition sessions, including any notice period required.
7. Communication: Specify the preferred method of communication between the tutor and parents, such as phone calls, emails, or an online platform.
8. Code of conduct: Emphasize the importance of maintaining a respectful and professional relationship between the tutor, student, and parents. Include guidelines on punctuality, dress code, confidentiality, and adherence to house rules.
9. Progress tracking: Explain how the tutor will track and communicate the student's progress to the parents, such as through regular progress reports or meetings.
10. Termination: State the conditions under which either party can terminate the agreement, along with any notice period required.
11. Liability and indemnity: Clarify the responsibilities and liability of both the tutor and the parents in case of any accidents or damages during the tuition sessions.
12. Confidentiality: Ensure that the tutor respects the privacy and confidentiality of the student and their family, refraining from sharing any personal information with third parties.
13. Dispute resolution: Provide a procedure for resolving any disputes or disagreements that may arise during the course of the tuition.
14. Agreement acceptance: Include a section for both the parents and the tutor to sign and date the agreement, indicating their understanding and acceptance of its terms.

Remember, it is always advisable to consult with a legal professional to ensure that the terms and conditions are suitable for your specific situation and comply with any local regulations or laws.
